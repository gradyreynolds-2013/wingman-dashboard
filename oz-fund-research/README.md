## Bryan's Strategy Summary

**Fund Structure:**
- Raise $10-30M from outside investors
- Co-invest significantly (aim for 50%+ of capital)
- Contribute development fees as equity (aligns incentives)

**Deployment:**
- 18-30 month deployment window
- 3-5 properties (portfolio diversification)
- Urban infill multifamily in growth markets
- Value-add renovation strategy

**Operations:**
- Professional property management
- Target 22-25% operating expense ratio
- 3% annual rent growth (conservative)
- Fixed debt service = predictable cash flow

**Refinancing:**
- Year 3-5 refinancing window
- 75% LTV based on stabilized NOI
- Return 60-75% of investor capital
- Maintain 100% equity ownership

**Exit:**
- Year 10 hold for 100% tax-free treatment
- 5-6% exit cap rate (conservative)
- Target 300%+ total return
- All gains tax-free for OZ investors
