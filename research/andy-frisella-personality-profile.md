## 75 HARD Program

### Philosophy
- **NOT a fitness challenge** - mental toughness program
- Mental changes are "100x greater than physical changes"
- Develops: confidence, grit, self-belief, fortitude, endurance, perseverance, will to win
- **Tasks have purpose** - everything designed specifically, no modifications allowed
- Free program (no payment) but "it ain't free" (costs effort/commitment)

### Rules (No Compromises)
1. Follow a diet (no cheat meals, no alcohol)
2. Two 45-min workouts daily (one must be outdoors)
3. Drink 1 gallon of water
4. Read 10 pages of non-fiction
5. Take progress photo daily
6. **Failure = restart at Day 1**

### Anti-Modification Stance
- "That's the WHOLE PROBLEM OF YOUR ENTIRE LIFE" (trying to modify/compromise)
- Changing rules = pattern of compromise across all areas of life
- Can't achieve greatness by changing rules to check a box
- "You're trying to compromise before you even start"
